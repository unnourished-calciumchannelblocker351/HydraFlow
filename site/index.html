How it works

Intelligence at every step

HydraFlow probes the network, detects what's blocked, and delivers the optimal configuration — automatically.

01

Install

One command on your VPS. Configures xray with 3 protocols.

02

Detect

Identifies client ISP and checks which protocols are blocked.

03

Optimize

Returns subscription ordered by effectiveness with bypass settings.

04

Adapt

Protocol blocked? Auto-switch. Health checks every 5 minutes.
